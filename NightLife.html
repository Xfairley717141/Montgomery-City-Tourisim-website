  • The Breakroom

    • The Breakroom is Montgomery's oldest pool hall in town and one of the best-kept secrets in the local nightlife scene. Our spacious spot is filled with the best pool tables in town in addition to darts and online games like trivia and poker, so there’s no shortage of things to compete with your friends over. Our awesome bartenders will set you up with unique, carefully crafted cocktails or a cold beer, and be sure to check out our menu for a selection of delicious wings, appetizers, pizzas, and sandwiches. Stop by for a friendly game with old and new friends or join a pool or dart league -- either way, you’re going to have an unforgettable time.
